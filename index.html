Top three activities to do at Canlubang

Explore the Canlubang Sugar Estate

Visitors can take a tour of the Canlubang Sugar Estate and learn about the sugar industry in the Philippines.

Play Golf at Canlubang Golf and Country Club

It is a premier golf course in the Philippines that offers a challenging and scenic golfing experience.

Experience Nature at Nuvali Evoliving Park

It is a nature park that offers a variety of outdoor activities such as biking, hiking, and fishing.
